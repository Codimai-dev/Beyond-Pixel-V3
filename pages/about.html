Our Story

Why Beyond Pixel exists

Beyond Pixel was born out of a simple observation: most AI projects fail because they start with technology, not business outcomes.

We saw companies buying expensive tools without ROI clarity, drowning in manual processes despite "automation," and building disconnected systems that created more noise than value. We decided to build a firm that puts business impact first.

Business-First AI Philosophy

We don't just build models; we build systems that solve specific problems. Our philosophy is rooted in three core pillars:

  • Outcome-Driven: Every line of code must serve a primary business KPI.
  • Clarity-Centered: We cut through the AI hype to provide simple, actionable intelligent solutions.
  • Ethical Implementation: Building with governance, privacy, and long-term scalability in mind.

Arpit Upadhyay Founder

Healthcare AI & Cardiac Technology Specialist

For the last 17+ years, Arpit has lived inside hospitals, cath labs, and diagnostics networks across India and Africa—working with cardiologists, administrators, and government programs to fix very real problems, not just deploy software.

He has led implementations in cardiac screening, tele-cardiology, and AI-enabled diagnostics, giving him a front-row view of long queues, burnout, leakages, and missed diagnoses. That experience is what shapes Beyond Pixel: every solution is designed to work in the chaos of real healthcare, with your clinicians and operations at the center.
